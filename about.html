About Us

Andi's Passion for Flowers

Andi has always loved to appreciate the beauty of every flower she encounters. From their vibrant colors to their unique fragrances, flowers have always captured her heart. She finds joy in how each bloom has its own personality, and it brings her happiness to share this beauty with others.

Her inspiration grew even further when she read the book "It Ends With Us" by Colleen Hoover. This book sparked something in Andi, and it was through reading it that she decided to follow her dream of opening her own floral shop. She wanted a place where she could combine her love for flowers with her passion for creating meaningful experiences for others.
